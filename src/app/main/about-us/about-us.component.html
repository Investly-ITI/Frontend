Our Story

Founded in 2023 at ITI Mansoura University, Investly was born from a simple observation: Egypt has an abundance of innovative entrepreneurs and forward-thinking investors, but they often struggle to find each other.

Our founders, having experienced this challenge firsthand, decided to build a platform that would bridge this gap. What started as a university project has now grown into Egypt's premier investment platform, facilitating millions of EGP in funding and helping hundreds of startups get off the ground.

Today, we're proud to be headquartered at ITI Mansoura University, where we continue to innovate and support the next generation of Egyptian entrepreneurs.
